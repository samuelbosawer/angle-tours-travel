About Us

Welcome to Angel Tours & Travel

Explore your dream destination and make unforgettable memories with us

Angel Tours & Travel is a professional travel service provider in Bali, offering comfortable, safe, and memorable travel experiences. With a team of experienced drivers and tour guides, we provide a selection of high-quality packages ranging from Half-Day Tours, Full-Day Tours, to adventure activities such as ATV and Rafting in Ubud.

Each journey is thoughtfully designed to take you through Bali’s rich culture, local craftsmanship, historic temples, and breathtaking natural scenery such as the Tegalalang Rice Terrace, Tegenungan Waterfall, and the beauty of Kintamani.
